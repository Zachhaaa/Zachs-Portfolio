FIRST 2023 Season

      2023 Game: Charged Up

      This year, you score points by placing cubes and cones onto platforms and poles. The team with the most points wins.

      You can check out the details in this video.

      Design

      The robot has to be able to pick up cubes and cones, which we accomplished with our 3 roller intake.

      This was our first year with a swerve drive, which allows movement in any direction at any orientation.

      Arm design

      The arm is double jointed which extends our reach and increases maneuverability.
