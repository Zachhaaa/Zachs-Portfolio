What is First

      FIRST is a robotics competition where high school students build robots and compete with them.

      How does it work?

      Every year, in early January, a new game is released and we have to design a robot that scores as many points as possible in that given game.

      What does a season look like?

      Once the game is released, our team carefully reads over the rules and starts to plan out designs.

      From there we have 8 weeks called "Build Season" to design, manufacture, and test our robot. After the 8 weeks competitions start and we compete with as many 60 teams in a competition.
