Wood Pen

      Shape / Design

      Since the metal parts are from a pen kit, I only made the wood barrels where you grab the pen.

      These were simple, as the wood blocks only needed to be drilled out and then cut down on the lathe.

      As intented, the surface came out smooth.

      Build Process

      1. Cut wood blocks down to size on a bandsaw
      2. Drill out the center of the block
      3. Use JB Weld to adhere the wood to the metal tube
      4. Bring down to size on the wood lathe
      5. Sand down the sides transitioning from low grit to high grit sandpaper as you go
      6. Apply a finish to make the wood glossy
      7. Assemble the pen
