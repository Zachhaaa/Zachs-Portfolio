What is Soldering?

      In soldering, metal is melted with a soldering iron and applied to a where an electrical connection is desired

      Soldering has many benefits including:

      • Reliability
      • High mechanical strength
      • Rust resistence
      • Low cost

      Almost all electronic devices today have a PCB with components soldered onto it.
