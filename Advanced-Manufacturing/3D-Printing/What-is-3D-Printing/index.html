What is 3D Printing

      3D printing makes parts by building them up layer by layer.

      There are a few types of 3D printing, but the type used in my project if called Fused Deposition Modeling or FDM for short.

      The term FDM doesn't really expain how it makes parts, but essentially, a strand of plastic enters a hot nozzle, the pastic melts, and the nozzle moves around the part to place the plastic in the correct areas. It does this layer by layer to form a 3D object.
